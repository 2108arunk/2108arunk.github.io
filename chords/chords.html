Ideology

I am learning to play guitar and it is so frustrating to search chords shapes online to find websites that do everything other than showing the guitar chord diagrams. So I made one for my own.

How to read the diagrams

The chord diagrams are presented in plain ascii. Each string is represented with its name. X - don't play that string O - play the string open Numeral (1...) - Play the string from that fret As simple as that.

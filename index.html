Well...here we go

Hey there, my name is Rohit and this is my website. You might wonder why does it looks so 1990s. That is because I intend it to be that way, making a simple website doesn't need to involve a dozen frameworks. I admire people like Luke Smith and appreciate philosophies of UNIX. Keeping things simple and avoiding the omnipresent over-engineering is the main motive here.
